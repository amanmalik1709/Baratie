About Us

Welcome to Baratie, the premier floating restaurant on the East Blue!



Baratie is renowned for its exquisite cuisine, exceptional service, and unique maritime atmosphere. Our establishment offers a dining experience unlike any other, where the wonders of the sea meet the delights of gourmet food.



Our menu features a wide array of gourmet seafood, succulent meats, and vibrant vegetarian options, all crafted with the finest ingredients and utmost care. Each dish is a culinary masterpiece designed to delight your senses, showcasing the skills and creativity of our talented chefs.



The ambiance at Baratie is a blend of nautical charm and elegant comfort. The floating structure provides breathtaking views of the surrounding waters, enhancing the dining experience with a serene and picturesque backdrop. The interior design reflects a harmonious mix of maritime elements and sophisticated decor, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.



Our dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional service, ensuring that every guest feels valued and well-cared for. Attention to detail, professionalism, and a genuine passion for hospitality are at the core of our service philosophy.



Baratie is not just a place to dine, but a destination where the love for fine food and the spirit of adventure come together. The unique setting, combined with our culinary excellence and top-notch service, makes Baratie a standout choice for those seeking a memorable dining experience on the East Blue.

Our Story

Baratie was founded by the legendary Red-Leg Zeff, a former pirate whose adventures on the high seas are as famous as his culinary prowess. After years of navigating the treacherous waters of the Grand Line, Zeff decided to anchor his passion for cooking and create a unique dining haven on the East Blue. Thus, Baratie was born—a floating restaurant where the spirit of adventure and the love for gourmet cuisine come together.



Zeff's journey from pirate to chef is a tale of resilience and transformation. Having experienced the vast and varied flavors of the world's oceans, he aimed to bring those tastes to life in a setting that captures the essence of maritime adventure. Baratie embodies Zeff's vision of a place where everyone, from pirates to marines to wandering travelers, can enjoy exceptional food and warm hospitality.



The floating restaurant itself is a marvel of design, offering a unique dining experience with stunning views of the surrounding waters. Its nautical-themed decor and cozy ambiance create a welcoming retreat for those seeking both adventure and comfort.



Baratie stands as a beacon of hospitality on the East Blue, where the legacy of Red-Leg Zeff lives on through the exceptional food and service that define this iconic establishment. It is a place where stories are shared, meals are savored, and the spirit of the sea is ever-present.
